Things to do in Sri Lanka

The Turtle Hatchery was started in Sri Lanka to protect the turtles from extinction. The hatcheries provide protection for different species of turtles.

If you are looking for an exciting and unique adventure, then a whale watching cruise from Galle to Mirissa Fishery Port is definitely the experience for you.

Take a plunge into white water rafting in Kitugala. With some of the wettest conditions in the country, you won't want to miss this adventure.

Going on a safari in Sri Lanka offers visitors an opportunity to view these magnificent creatures up close as they roam their natural habitats.

Experience the beauty of Sri Lanka's Indian Ocean waters! From majestic Blue Whales to tiny nudibranchs, a variety of marine life awaits you.
